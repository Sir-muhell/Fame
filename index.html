What we do

EMPOWERING FARMERS

FAME provides a WEB3 social marketplace that empowers social upliftment through mobile technology and financial access for workers, drivers and farmers in Africa.

ADOPTING A HOLISTIC APPROACH

Drivers are essential to the organic growth of our FAME ecosytem so we let them freely post their avaibilities and costs.

UNLOCKING OPPORTUNITIES

An estimated 500 million smallholder farming families rely to varying degrees on agricultural production for their livelihoods, while also contributing up to 70% of the African continent's food supply.

EMPOWERING FAMILIES

The FAME APP allows free remittances all round the world, which reprensents 20 billion$/year in Nigerai alone. The African diaspora can now contribute directly to help their families jsut from our CHAT PAY.

JOB MARKETPLACE

As workers increasingly gain access to smartphones, they can easily scroll down the FAME job marketplace, accept or ignore certain offers, knowing they can apply a certain leverage to improve their livelihood.
